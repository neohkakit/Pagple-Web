Introducing Pagple


Re-discover. Re-connect. Re-form everything you ever thought of the common marketplace.


Pagple aims to connect buyers and sellers on a different scale of user-experience. The simplest way to connect and share with brands, buyers, sellers and bloggers on three platfoms at a global scale. We believe the world deserve a platform where brands are ever closer to us; and the ability to purchase anything at ease from the people we love.

Three


Just three platforms. Fashion, Technology and Food.


Three of a kind


“Three things cannot be long hidden: the sun, the moon and the truth - Buddha. The truth is, we think having 10-20 platforms only brings more complication. We decided to stick to the only three platforms that really matters to you.


Simple

Simplicity is the ultimate complexity.

Less is simply more


One of our main principal is absolute simplicity.
Pagple will be the simplest and easiest place to connect brands you love, share interesthings and sell goods.

When we started building Pagple; we had tonnes of features that struck our mind but implementing all
would just kill the simplistic user-experience therefore, reducing everything to minimal makes it all better to
use. Our navigation bar fits the explanation.
